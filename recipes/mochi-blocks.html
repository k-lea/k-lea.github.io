Mochi Blocks


Cooking Instructions

Heat toaster oven to 375 degrees F.

Place unwrapped block on a pan or tinfoil. Watch it until it has popped and is about twice its size.

Meanwhile, boil about 2" of water.

Mix brown sugar with a few drops of soy sauce and water.

Put puffed rice cake in boiling water for about 5 seconds and transfer to bowl with sauce.
