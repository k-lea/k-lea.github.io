Pumpkin Ginger Snap Dip


Ingredients

  • 4 oz Cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup Powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup Heavy whipping cream
  • 3/4 cup Pumpkin
  • 2 tsp Pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 Tbsp Maple syrup
  • Gingersnaps

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cream together cream cheese and powdered sugar until smooth.
  2. Blend in cream.
  3. Stir in spices.
  4. Refrigerate until serving. Serve with gingersnaps.
