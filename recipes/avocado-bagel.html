Avocado Bagel


Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/8 tsp Pepper
  • 1 Bagel
  • 1 Egg
  • Butter

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mash avocado and stir in salt and pepper.
  2. Enlarge the hole in both halves of the bagel to at least 1 1/2 inches.
  3. Spread butter on cut sides of bagel.
  4. Place bagel, butter side down, in a skillet and cook 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Spread avocado over browned sides.
  6. Return bagel to skillet, avocado sides up. Crack an egg into the center of each bagel half.
  7. Cook until eggs are at desired doneness.
